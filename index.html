Welcome to my How-To guide for Ravelry's API. This guide will help you get started using the Ravelry API. I will attempt to fill gaps in the documentation that currently exists on the internet.

Ravelry.com is a social media site for knitters, crocheters, and fiber artists. It offers tools for planning and tracking projects, discussion forums, interest groups, a fully searchable catalog of designs and patterns (free and for purchase), a yarn stash inventory, and more!

Using the Ravelry API, you can retrieve information from Ravelry to put in your blog or social media site. You can create cool apps and widgets such as a project counter button, this progress bar , or this flash widget . You can even design a whole new app like this one!
